Easy Start: High School Algebra

Design an algebraic expression simplifier which takes two polynomial expressions and prints their product in the specified form.

Algebraic expressions will be expressed according to following rules:

1)      The terms of the expressions are in decreasing order of powers.

2)      The convention for typical form of the  expression will be as follows:

 anx^pn-1+an-1x^pn-2+..........+a1p0+a0  (where ai are coefficients and pi are the powers)

eg.:  3x^2+5x^1+1

3)      The algebraic expressions are to be expressed in their full expanded form.

Input Format:

Line 1: Algebraic Expression 1

Line 2: Algebraic Expression 2

 

Output Format:

Line 1: Product of the expressions

 

Sample Input:

x^2+x

x^1+1

 

Sample Output:

x^3+2x^2+x
